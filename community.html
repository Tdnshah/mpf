Community

Building a Strong Community of Partnerships

Bridging the gap between the youth and organizations that can help youth make a difference has always been our topmost agenda. Over the years, MASH Project Foundation has partnered with various organizations, corporations, NGOs, and CSOs in both the public and private sectors to maximise the impact and influence of its projects and programmes across the world. These partnerships not only provide us support but also help us in running our programmes more effectively and efficiently. Increasingly, organisations have also been recognising the importance of value creation through social interventions along with business efforts.
