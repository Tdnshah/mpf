#28For28

28 Climate Changemakers. 28 Stories. #28For28 campaign is empowering the youth to lead action on climate change by showcasing the voices and stories of 28 young climate changemakers through open-mic events leading up to COP28.

Shape the future with #28for28

Why#28 For 28

Climate Changemakers

Bringing together climate changemakers from across the country to create a ripple effect of climate action led by the youth.

Building Momentum

Mobilizing a movement of changemakers who are committed to the cause of climate action through art expression

Fostering Innovation

Fostering innovation by placing climate action at the front and center of storytelling, art expression, community-building, and advocacy.

Catalyzing Progress

Catalyzing progress by featuring impactful initiatives of climate changemakers so they have a seat at the table and a say in shaping the future.

Our Supporters

We Are Honoured To Have Partners Who Are Leading The Way In Climate Action. From India To Canada And Beyond, These Partners Are Committed To Championing Youth-Led Climate Solutions And Inspiring The Next Generation Of Changemakers.

Let's Hear What They Have To Say!

Be a Part of #28For28

At the heart of our campaign lies empowering young changemakers to be part of the discourse on climate change. We want them to foster a sense of agency and purpose, igniting a movement that can drive real change toward a sustainable future.

Share your own stories, ideas, or creative expressions related to climate change on social media using #28For28. Stand a chance to be featured and invited to events!
